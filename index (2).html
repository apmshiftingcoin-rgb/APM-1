FAQs

Common Questions, Answered

How is the pricing for a move calculated?
Pricing depends on the volume of goods, distance, floor access and any additional services like packing or storage. We always share a detailed, itemised quote before the move begins.
Is my shipment insured during transit?
Yes, we offer transit insurance options for added peace of mind on both household and office relocations. Our team will explain coverage details at the time of booking.
What packing materials do you use?
We use multi-ply cartons, bubble wrap, foam sheets, wooden crating for fragile items, and waterproof covers, all suited to the specific items being moved.
How do I book a move with APM?
Simply call us, WhatsApp, or fill the enquiry form on this page. We'll arrange a quick assessment and share a quote, usually within the same day.
How long does delivery usually take?
Local moves within Delhi NCR are typically completed within the same day. Intercity and pan-India moves vary by distance, generally between 2-7 days, and are confirmed upfront.
Do you transport bikes and cars too?
Yes, we provide secure vehicle transportation using carriers and trailers suited to two-wheelers and cars, with careful loading and securing procedures.
Do you offer short-term storage?
Yes, our warehouse facilities support both short-term and long-term storage, with secure, monitored conditions for your goods.
Can you handle large office relocations?
Yes, we plan office moves around your working hours to minimise downtime, including IT equipment, furniture and document handling.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, bank transfer, major cards and cash, with receipts provided for every payment.
What safety measures do you follow during a move?
Every item is wrapped and labelled, fragile goods are crated separately, and our drivers follow GPS-tracked routes so you always know the status of your shipment.
